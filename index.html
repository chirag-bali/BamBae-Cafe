Who we are

Our Story

Redefining café culture in Panchkula with wholesome, vibrant living.

The Vision

A Fresh Take on
Asian Comfort

BamBae was born out of a desire to bring clean, vibrant and incredibly flavorful food to the Tricity. Nestled inside the beautiful Welcomhotel Bella Vista in Sector 5, Panchkula, we created a space that feels like a genuine oasis.

Inspired by health-conscious café culture and authentic Asian street flavors, our menu is an extensive fusion — from crispy Bánh Mì baguettes and Vietnamese dripped coffees to protein-packed shakes and towering Biscoff pancakes.

Every ingredient is sourced fresh. Every recipe is crafted with intention. We're proud to be Panchkula's go-to destination for eating well and feeling good.

Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer Vegan or Gluten-Free options?
Yes! Many of our Smoothie Bowls, Teas, and Salads are naturally vegan and gluten-free. Please let our staff know about your dietary preferences when ordering, and we will happily accommodate you.
Is there parking available at the café?
Absolutely. Since we are located inside Welcomhotel Bella Vista, ample and secure valet parking is available for all our guests visiting the café.
Do you take bulk orders or cater for events?
We certainly do! We regularly cater to corporate lunches, birthdays, and private parties. Please reach out to us via our WhatsApp Support to discuss custom packages.
Can I order online for home delivery?
Yes, you can order directly through Zomato or Swiggy for delivery across the Tricity area. You can also place an order here on the website for self-pickup or dine-in.
